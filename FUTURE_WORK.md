# Future Work

A running backlog of ideas and tasks deliberately deferred to a later plan, rather
than done now. When something is decided to be "for the future", it is recorded here
instead of being lost in conversation.

## How to use this file

- Add an entry whenever work is consciously postponed (out of scope, blocked, or
  "nice to have later").
- Keep each entry short: what to do, and why it was deferred.
- Move an item to `## Done` (or delete it) once it ships.
- Newest items go to the top of the relevant section.

## Format

```
- [ ] <Short title> — <what & why deferred>. (added YYYY-MM-DD)
```
